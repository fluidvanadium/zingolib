Zingo! A Zcash Application

Use zero knowledge cryptography to manage your transaction history in a private and mindful way.

Take exclusive and unique control over your history. Use this unique insight into your
own financial behavior to introspect on how you spend money. Optionally, share
insights on your terms. Establish healthy relationships with diverse economic communities.
Only you have the power to share your information! Your perspective matters. You matter.

Zingo! uses on-chain memos to store information associated with your transactions. The client 
extracts that data to provide insights exclusively to you.  Your data is never exposed to any
third party.  By providing Users with private insight into their own economic behavior, we
are enabling everyone to make healthier financial desisions. We anticipate diverse communities
that form around different kinds of insights, to collaborate on shared values.
